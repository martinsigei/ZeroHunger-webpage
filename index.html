Our Stories

ZeroHunger Logistics is committed to transforming the food supply chain in Kenya . Our goal is to ensure that fresh, nutritious food is accessible to all by directly connecting farmers with consumers and traders through innovative technology.

Launched to address the challenges of food distribution in Kenya, ZeroHunger Logistics bridges the gap between local farmers and the market. Our platform is designed to empower farmers, reduce food waste, and provide consumers with reliable access to fresh groceries.

Our Story

We envision a world where every table is served with high-quality food, waste is minimized, and food security is achieved through a seamless, efficient supply chain.
